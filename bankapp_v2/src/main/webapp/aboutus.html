Syed’s Bank is the largest nationalised bank in India headquartered in Hyderabad, Telangana. It is under the ownership of Ministry of Finance , Government of India. Syed’s Bank is the 55th largest bank in the world and ranked 221st in the Fortune Global 500 list of the world's biggest corporations of 2020, being the only Indian bank on the list.It is the largest nationalised bank in India with a 23% market share by assets and a 25% share of the total loan and deposits market. It is also the fifth largest employer in India with nearly 250,000 employees.
